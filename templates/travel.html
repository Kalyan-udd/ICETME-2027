How to Reach Us

flight

By Air

The nearest airport is Maharaja Bir Bikram (MBB) Airport in Agartala (IXA), located about 25 km from the NIT Agartala campus. Direct flights are available from major cities like Delhi, Kolkata, Guwahati, and Bangalore.

train

By Train

Agartala Railway Station (AGTL) is the main junction, well-connected to the rest of India. The local Jirania Railway Station (JRNA) is extremely close to the campus (approx. 3 km) for regional connectivity.

directions_car

By Road

Agartala is connected via National Highway 8 (NH-8). Pre-paid taxis, auto-rickshaws, and buses are readily available from the airport, railway station, and city center to the NIT Agartala campus.

Accommodation Options

apartment Inside Campus

Limited accommodation is available at the NIT Agartala Guest House and selected hostels on a first-come, first-served basis. Scholars and students may request campus lodging during registration.

hotel City Hotels

Agartala offers a range of excellent hotels suited for all budgets (e.g., Hotel Polo Towers, Hotel Sonar Tori). The campus is an easy 30-40 minute drive from the main city center.
