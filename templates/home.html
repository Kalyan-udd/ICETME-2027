ICETME–2027 serves as a premier global forum for researchers, academicians, and industry leaders to discuss breakthroughs in mechanical engineering.

Our goal is to foster deep collaboration across the globe, merging cutting-edge theoretical research with realistic industrial scale applications. Participate in keynote sessions, paper presentations, and extensive networking opportunities to drive the future of engineering.

Call for Papers

We invite original research contributions in the following domains. Shape the narrative of engineering by presenting your research.

Track 1: Smart Materials, Advanced Manufacturing & Sustainable Production Systems

Advanced materials, composites, biomaterials, materials characterization, powder metallurgy, surface engineering, conventional and non-conventional manufacturing (machining, welding, casting, forming), micro- and nano-manufacturing, digital manufacturing, Industry 4.0/5.0, CAD/CAM, product lifecycle management, product design, supply chain management, maintenance engineering, reliability and quality engineering. Engineering mechanics, structural analysis, computational mechanics, modelling and simulation, optimization, machine learning and AI applications, vibrations, multi-body dynamics, tribology, fatigue and failure analysis, machines and mechanisms, robotics, mechatronics, sensors and actuators, control systems, UAVs/MAVs, avionics, aerospace structures, flight mechanics, testing and simulation, uncertainty quantification.
