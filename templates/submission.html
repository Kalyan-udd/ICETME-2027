Author Guidelines

Paper Submission.

Please follow the two-step process below to successfully submit your manuscript and complete your registration for the conference.

upload_file
Step 1

Initial Paper Submission

All authors are required to submit their full papers electronically through the official Microsoft CMT (Conference Management Toolkit) portal. Please ensure your manuscript adheres to the conference formatting guidelines before uploading.

fact_check
Step 2

Post-Acceptance Registration

After your paper has been officially accepted, attendees must complete their registration and finalize their participation by filling out the required Google Form.

list_alt Required Details for Form:

  • person Name
  • title Title of the paper
  • tag Paper ID (Generated via CMT)
  • account_balance Institution
  • call Contact Number
  • mail Email ID
  • receipt_long Transaction ID (Fee payment)
  • video_camera_front Mode of participation (Online/Physical)
